Roar Voice

Jaffna Roar Media is the vibrant voice of the Faculty of Technology. We are dedicated to enhancing communication, promoting technological innovations and celebrating the achievements of our students and faculty through creative media solutions. Our mission is to engage, inspire and inform the University and the wider community by amplifying the stories of innovation, research and academic excellence that define our Faculty.

Vision

To be the cornerstone of digital communication and media innovation in the Faculty of Technology.

Mission

To provide high-quality media services, foster media literacy and support the University's outreach and engagement goals.

Core Services

Photography, Videography, Graphic Design, Content Strategy, Live Streaming and Event Organization.

Our Family...!

We are a passionate collective of students, faculty and media professionals united by the goal of elevating the Faculty of Technology's presence across digital platforms and the broader community. Our family is guided by creativity, collaboration and commitment.
